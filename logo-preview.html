How to choose

  • 🅐 Heading — Best if you want minimal, precise, and timeless. Works on any background. Feels professional and understated.
  • 🅑 Waypoint — Best if you want bold and instantly memorable. The filled blue badge creates strong visual weight in headers. Typical of modern SaaS products.
  • 🅒 Chevrons — Best if you want energetic and distinctive. The three-chevron silhouette is highly recognisable and communicates "moving forward" and "multiple workflows."
