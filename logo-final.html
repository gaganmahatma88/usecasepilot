Icon Anatomy
① Rounded-square badge 28×28 · rx=8 · #2563EB — solid, no gradient. Creates a strong, contained brand mark.
② Origin dot r=3 · white · bottom-left. Represents the user's starting point — "I am here."
③ Geometric L-route Vertical → 90° rounded corner → horizontal. Represents a structured, guided path. The corner radius uses a Q bezier for a smooth, precision feel.
④ Solid arrowhead Filled triangle pointing right. Bold destination marker. Readable even at 16px.
